How to open the Tableau dashboard:
1. Create a database called "testglobalfoodprices"
2. Import the DB dump into the database "testglobalfoodprices". The MySQL DB dump folder name is "GlobalFoodPricesDbDump"
3. Open the Tableau workbook called "MarketPricesOfFoodItemsInSouthEastAsis.twb"
4. You will be promted to provide the MySql "testglobalfoodprices" db credentials. Fill in the details and the dashboard would open in front of you.
5. Just looking at the dashboard, we can easily and quickly find out the lowest price of various food items such as rice/wheat/lentils/ptatoes/onions in various neighboring countries and the provinces.
